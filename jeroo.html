Jeroo Unit 1

In unit 1 of Jeroo, we learned about the Jeroos that are stranded on an island with water on all for sides. We learned how to create Jeroos and code basic movements of the Jeroos.

Concepts Learned:

  • Creating a new Jeroo - Jeroo bob = new Jeroo
  • Moving - hop(Count);
  • Plant a flower - plant();
  • Pick - pick();
  • Toss a flower - toss();
  • Give a flower - give(dir);
  • Turn in a direction - turn(dir);
